Elemental Warfare (v1.4) — Weapon Balance Reference
=============================================

Source: common/component_templates/ew_components.txt (inline stats)
Compared against vanilla Stellaris weapon_components.csv and component templates.


Overview
--------

All five elements share identical base damage, fire rate, range, and power per slot
size. Element choice only changes hull / shield / armor damage multipliers.

Tech unlocks:

  Elemental Theory (tier 2, physics)
    Requires: tech_plasma_1 (Plasma Throwers I)
    Chain: Red Lasers → Blue Lasers → Plasma I → Elemental Theory

  Element tier 1 (after Elemental Theory) — S, M, L, P slots
  Element tier 2 — G (torpedo), H (strike craft)
  Element tier 3 — X (spinal), T (titan beam)

Overall: EW weapons sit around mid-to-late vanilla energy-weapon damage numbers.
Elemental Theory opens after Plasma I (vanilla tier 2). Power draw is far lower
than equivalent vanilla weapons. Accuracy is slightly lower than standard lasers
(0.82 vs 0.90 on batteries).

Slot-by-slot vanilla comparison
-------------------------------

S (Small Battery)
  EW: 16–42, power −2, cooldown 30
  Vanilla reference:
    X-Ray Laser:     13–35,  power −13
    Plasma II:       16–43,  power −17, cooldown 35
    Gamma Laser:     17–46,  power −17, cooldown 30
  Verdict: Between X-Ray and Plasma II / Gamma in raw damage, with laser fire rate
           and much lower power cost.

M (Medium Battery)
  EW: 40–106, power −5, cooldown 35
  Vanilla reference:
    X-Ray Laser:     33–88,  power −30
    Plasma II:       40–108, power −36, cooldown 40
    Gamma Laser:     43–115, power −39, cooldown 35
  Verdict: Essentially Plasma II damage at laser cadence. Power −5 vs Plasma II −36.

L (Large Battery)
  EW: 96–252, power −10, cooldown 40
  Vanilla reference:
    X-Ray Laser:     78–210,  power −67
    Plasma II:       96–258,  power −82, cooldown 45
    Gamma Laser:     102–276, power −88, cooldown 40
  Verdict: Just under Plasma II / Gamma. Power −10 vs Plasma II −82.

P (Point Defense)
  EW: 8–16, power −3, cooldown 5
  Vanilla reference:
    Point Defense III: 4–8
    Flak Battery IV:   6–11
  Verdict: Strong PD per shot — above top-tier flak and point defense.

G (Torpedo)
  EW: 200–300, power −8, cooldown 150 (instant torpedo, not missile)
  Vanilla reference:
    Energy Torpedo I:  47–101
    Energy Torpedo II: 61–131
    Kinetic Torpedo III: 169–254
  Verdict: Well above energy torpedoes; comparable to high-tier kinetic torpedoes.
           Tier 2 unlock.

H (Strike Craft)
  EW: 20–44 per craft, 8 craft, cooldown 2.5, power −10
  Vanilla reference:
    Strike Craft I:   4–8
    Strike Craft II:  5–13
    Strike Craft III: 6–17
  Verdict: Far above Strike Craft III per craft. Tier 2 unlock.

X (Spinal Lance)
  EW: 1200–3000, power −35, cooldown 70
  Vanilla reference:
    Energy Lance I:    600–1500,  power −200
    Energy Lance II:   780–1950,  power −260
    Mass Accelerator I: 700–2000, power −200
    Arc Emitter I:     1–1300 (high variance)
  Verdict: Between Energy Lance I and II on average; high rolls approach Mass
           Accelerator territory. Power −35 vs Lance −200/−260. Tier 3 unlock.

T (Titan Beam)
  EW: 5000–10000, power −75, cooldown 180, range 250
  Vanilla reference:
    Titan Laser: 5000–10000, power −500, cooldown 180, range 250
  Verdict: Identical damage, timing, and range to Titan Laser. EW power draw in
           ew_components.txt is −75 (vanilla CSV lists −500 for TITAN_LASER).
           Tier 3 unlock.

Practical notes
---------------

1. Tier 1 is strong for unlock timing
   Plasma-tier punch at laser fire rates after Plasma I, Elemental Theory, and
   one element tier-1 tech. Elemental weapons are a step up from Plasma I stats.

2. Power efficiency is a major hidden advantage
   Same-ish damage at a fraction of vanilla power — more weapons or utilities on
   the same hull.

3. Element choice is matchup, not base DPS
   Shields  → Lightning (1.45 shield mult)
   Armor    → Acid (1.55 armor mult) or Ice (1.25)
   Hull     → Fire (1.25 hull mult)
   Mixed    → Chaos (1.15 all)

4. Accuracy tradeoff
   EW batteries use 0.82 accuracy vs 0.90 on standard lasers — slightly more
   damage lost to misses.

5. Alloy costs (EW vs vanilla, approximate)
   S: 20 alloys    |  UV Laser S: 17
   M: 45 alloys    |  Plasma II M: 69
   L: 90 alloys    |  Plasma II L: 164
   X: 250 alloys   |  Energy Lance I: 250
   T: 500 alloys   |  Titan Laser: 500
